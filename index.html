Welcome to Louisville

InstructureCon 2026 is almost here, and we can’t wait to welcome you to Louisville, Kentucky.

Whether you’re coming for the sessions, the speakers, the networking, or simply the chance to explore what’s next in teaching, learning, and technology, this guide will help you get ready for a great week.

Think of it as your go-to prep resource for planning your schedule, navigating the conference, and making the most of your time at InstructureCon.

Download the app

The Instructure Events app is your home base for everything happening at InstructureCon. To get started:

  1. Download Instructure Events from the Apple App Store or Google Play Store (or search “Instructure Events” in your app store).
  2. Log in using the email address you used to register and select Send email link.
  3. Set up your profile with your name, title, organization, and photo.
  4. Add your LinkedIn profile so other attendees can connect with you.
  5. Use the Networking tab for 1:1 messaging and networking.
  6. If you don’t already have a Community account, the app will prompt you to join.

Not seeing InstructureCon 2026 in the app? Double check that you’re using the email address you registered with, then uninstall and reinstall the app.

Local logistics

Prepare for summer weather

Louisville in July is warm, sunny, and humid. Expect temperatures in the upper 80s and low 90s throughout the week.

A few recommendations:

  • Bring comfortable walking shoes.
  • Wear lightweight, breathable clothing.
  • Bring a refillable water bottle and stay hydrated.
  • Consider bringing a light layer for air-conditioned session rooms and meeting spaces.

Downtown travel

The ongoing I-65 Central Corridor Project may impact travel times around downtown Louisville, especially during peak hours. Build in a little extra time if you’re driving or using rideshare services.

Here’s the specific information:

Closure Location: Interstate 65 between Jefferson Street and Interstate 264 (Watterson Expressway)—this is the heavily used interstate for guests traveling to the Kentucky International Convention Center.
Closure date: June 1–July 31, 2026
Detours: I-65 traffic will be detoured using I-264 (Watterson Expressway) and Interstate 64, while on-ramps at Jefferson Street and Liberty Street/Muhammad Ali Boulevard will remain open.

Filter the agenda

There are hundreds of sessions at InstructureCon, so filters are your friend. To find content:

  1. Open the Agenda page on the web or in the app.
  2. Select the Filter button.
  3. Choose the Segment filter.
  4. Select your segment.

While you’re there, you can also filter by:

  • Session format
  • Instructure product
  • Skill level

Once you’ve found sessions you’re interested in, log in and star them to build your personal schedule.

See Canvas Career in action

Canvas Career is a skills-based learning platform built for professional learners. It’s designed for organizations that need training tied to real job skills, not just compliance requirements.

Here’s where to find it at InstructureCon

  • Demo booth: Open all three days, July 21–23, with walkthroughs of Skillspace, the IgniteAI Agent, and the Admin Dashboard
  • Customer sessions: Teams using Canvas Career today, including USA Swimming and White’s Chapel Methodist Church, will share how they’re using it
  • Pre-con workshops: A hands-on introduction to the platform ahead of the main conference

Check the agenda for exact times and locations.

Join the Community Hub

InstructureCon may last a few days, but the conversation doesn’t end with the closing night party.

Think of the InstructureCon Community Hub as the digital campus of the conference—a place to meet people before you arrive, find your people while you’re here, and keep the conversations going long after you head home.

Need a restaurant recommendation? Looking for someone to grab coffee with before a session? Wondering whether Hack Night is for beginners? The Community (and our Community Coaches) have your back.

Inside the Hub, you’ll find

  • Light Up the Map: See where attendees are traveling from
  • Details on Unconference, Hack Night, Game Night, and the Solo Pandas Meet Up
  • Session previews, presenter tips, and attendee recommendations
  • Packing lists and practical conference advice
  • The official InstructureCon Spotify playlist
  • Instructions for claiming your digital attendee badge
  • Community session blogs and highlights
  • FAQs, updates, and event support resources

Whether you’re planning your week, looking for your next session, or reliving your favorite moments after the event, the Community Hub is the place to be.
